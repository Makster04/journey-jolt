About:

  1. POINTS OF INTEREST: Greece offers a rich tapestry of attractions, from the iconic Acropolis in Athens and ancient ruins of Delphi to the stunning beaches of Santorini, vibrant nightlife, and delectable Mediterranean cuisine.
  2. CUSINE: Greek cuisine is a flavorful blend of olive oil, feta cheese, fresh vegetables, and herbs. Gyros, moussaka, souvlaki, and baklava showcase the diverse, delicious offerings of Mediterranean gastronomy.
  3. ECONOMY: Greece's economy, once strained, has seen improvements. It relies on tourism, shipping, agriculture, and services. Ongoing reforms and EU support contribute to its recovery from past financial challenges.
